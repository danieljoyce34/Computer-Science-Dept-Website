Activities

Students in the Department of Computing Sciences are encouraged to make the most of the following opportunities. Follow the links to learn more.

Community Outreach
is dedicated to various service projects happening in the department.
Upsilon Pi Epsilon (UPE)
is the Villanova chapter of the international honor society for the computing and information disciplines.
Alice Workshop
The program is a part of the series of events held by the Girls Scouts of Eastern Pennsylvania.
Programming Teams
competes in the ACM International Collegiate Programming Contest, also known as the "Battle of the Brains," which challenges students to solve real-world computer programming problems under a grueling, five-hour deadline.
Student Travel
Students who wish to attend conferences may be able to receive financial support for travel and conference costs.
