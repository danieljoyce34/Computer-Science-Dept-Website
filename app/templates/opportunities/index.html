Jobs and Internships

The CSC Department is committed to helping you find employment as a computing professional. The Villanova Career Services office is a valuable resource. In addition, the CSC department has its own resources, many specific to computing and our students.

While you are a student here, you may be able to work for the CSC department in one of several capacities. We also maintain a list of employment postings that are specific to computing and include opportunities that you might not see anywhere else. If you are specifically looking for an internship, our internship postings page maintains a list of employment opportunities specific for those companies looking for interns.

Villanova's Computing Sciences and Communications departments have a special agreement with the Vatican in Rome to provide student interns who will enhance the Vatican's presence on the internet. You can find out more information about this internship by visiting the Vatican Internship page.

There are many websites dedicated to aid students finding full-time, part-time and internship level jobs. Among these job seeking sites are Monster, Career Builder and Job Application Center. The ACM also maintains a great site dedicated to computing careers.

As you prepare for your job search, please take advantage of our pages designed to help you develop an effective resume and prepare you for effective interviews.
