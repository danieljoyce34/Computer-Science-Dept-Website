Faculty and Staff

We know that computing is really about people. Some of the best make up our faculty and staff.

The full-time faculty in our department are deeply committed to our mission of excellence in pedagogy and research. Their diversity and experience provide a rich environment for growth and learning.

Most of our adjunct faculty are computing professionals from the high-tech corridor surrounding Villanova. Their contributions in teaching, and their collaboration with our full-time faculty on various projects, bring additional insight from the commercial world into our classrooms.

The Department of Computing Sciences is supported by an excellent adminstrative and technical support staff who make the daily activities run smoothly.

In addition to teaching, our faculty members are busy helping the department and university through their work on numerous committees and serving the academic community and computing science disciplines.
