Graduate Grand Challenges - Awards

The Graduate Grand Challenges Award was instituted in 2001. Since that time, each Spring, the department Graduate Committee selects one outstanding Grand Challenges project for an award. All Grand Challenges projects completed in the previous calendar year are eligible for the award. The award is announced during the spring departmental graduation ceremony. In addition to the citation in the departmental graduation brochure, the awardee receives a nice plaque.
