MSCS and MSSE Program Application Guidelines

How to apply

    Applicants to the Degree Program should submit the following materials:

    • Application: Complete the online application form.
    • Application fee: Follow the instructions you receive during your application process. The fee may be waived for students with a Villanova degree or for students registering during our graduate open house.
    • Transcripts: Official transcripts of all previous college work (undergraduate and graduate where applicable). One copy of each. Applicants in their last year of undergraduate study who have not completed their bachelor's degree should provide an official transcript of all courses completed at the time of application, and submit another official transcript when the undergraduate degree is completed.
    • Three Letters of Recommendation: Please use the form found on the online application.
    • Graduate Record Examination: Please note that GRE scores from tests taken more than five years before the date of application will not be accepted. GMAT scores are acceptable as a substitute for GRE. GRE requirement may be waived for applicants with substantial professional working experience.

    If you have any questions you may contact Dr. Vijay Gehlot, the Program Director, at gradcomputing@villanova.edu or 610-519-7310.
