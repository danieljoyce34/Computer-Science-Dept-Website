Other Academic Programs

In addition to our accredited undergradate BS in Computer Science degree and our graduate MS degrees in Computer Science and in Software Engineering, the Department of Computing Sciences offers or supports a variety of other programs:

  • A 5-year Integrated program
  • A minor in Computer Science
  • Fluency in Information Technology Certificate
  • A concentration or minor in Cognitive Science
  • A major or certificate in Information Systems for adult learners
  • A major in Media and Technology for adult learners
  • Several courses for non-majors
    • CSC 1000 - The Practice of Computing
    • CSC 1010 - Programming for All
    • CSC 1020 - Computing and the Web
    • CSC 1024 - Computing for Scientists
    • CSC 1030 - Problem Solving with Computers
    • CSC 1035 - Databases for Many Majors
    • CSC 1040 - Computing with Images
    • CSC 1043 - Laptop Instrument
    • CSC 1045 - Algorithms, Cartoons, and Animation
